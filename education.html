02. College Stories // the unofficial transcript

🔄

The Great Branch Slide

Entered NIT Raipur with Mechanical on the form, ended up in Electrical. Sometimes the best routes are the detours — this one led to code, circuits, and zero regrets.

💡

E-Cell Days

Part of the Entrepreneurship Cell — brainstorming startup ideas, attending pitch competitions, and learning that "disrupting the industry" requires more than just a cool name.

💻

Coding Contests with Friends

Late-night competitive programming sessions in the hostel. Debugging each other's code, arguing about approaches, and celebrating accepted solutions like we won the World Cup.

🌙

All-Night Exam Prep

The classic hostel tradition: one night, 6 months of syllabus, endless cups of chai, and the unshakeable belief that "I'll start studying early next time." Spoiler: we never did.

🍔

The Hotel Friends Group

Every NIT student has that one food group. Ours had a dedicated WhatsApp group for ordering, heated debates about biryani rankings, and a shared tab that nobody wanted to settle.

🎂

Birthday Celebrations

NIT Raipur style — cake, chaos, and traditions that probably shouldn't be documented. What happens in the hostel stays in the hostel (mostly).

🎨

Face Painting & Fun

Cultural fests, face painting sessions, and activities that had absolutely nothing to do with engineering. The kind of memories that make you miss college at 2 AM on a Wednesday.
