My Story

The people, places, and chapters that shaped me

A personal journal — because every developer has an origin story

November 13, 2002

Chapter 1: The Beginning

Born in Pathak Nursing Home, Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. The world got a little noisier that day. Soon after, the family moved to Chirimiri — a small coal town in Korba district, where the real story began.

Chapter 2: School Days at KV Chirimiri

Kendriya Vidyalaya Chirimiri became my second home for 12 years (2008-2020). From Class 1st to 12th, CBSE board. Morning assemblies, inter-house competitions, and an unhealthy obsession with winning. Topped the school in Class 12th — the first plot twist in a life full of them.

Chapter 3: The NIT Raipur Chapter

Got into NIT Raipur for B.Tech. Started in Mechanical, slid into Electrical Engineering — turns out the best paths are the ones you don't plan. From circuits to code, the transformation was underway.

Chapter 4: The Software Developer

Joined Hypergro.ai as a Software Developer Engineer. Building backends, training LLMs, and solving problems at the intersection of AI and mathematics. The journey from Chirimiri to Bangalore — still in progress.
